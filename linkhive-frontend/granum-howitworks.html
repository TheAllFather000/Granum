What does Granum cost?

Short answer: almost nothing. We make money when suppliers make money — not before.

For spaza shop owners
R0

Free to use. Always.

Browsing, ordering, and managing your account costs nothing. You pay only for the products you order — at the listed price.

  • No subscription fee
  • No listing fee
  • No transaction fee
  • Delivery charged at R25 flat per order
For farmers & manufacturers
5%

Commission per sale only.

We charge 5% of the sale value only when an order is successfully delivered. No upfront cost, no monthly fee.

  • Free to create a supplier account
  • Free to list products
  • 5% deducted automatically from payout
  • Payout within 24hrs of delivery confirmation
For delivery drivers
Earn

Per-delivery income.

Drivers are paid per completed delivery. Rate depends on distance and order size. No fuel deductions — that's your business.

  • Earnings from R35–R120 per delivery
  • Weekly Friday payouts
  • Bonus for high-rated drivers
